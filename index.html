About Cornerstone

Cornerstone is a technology-driven platform focused on turning ideas into real-world solutions.

We specialize in IoT, Embedded Systems, Robotics, and AI with a strong focus on practical learning.

We believe in learning by doing and helping innovators build impactful projects.

Meet the Founder

Singaram Kiran

Founder of Cornerstone

Singaram Kiran is a district-level Innovation Coordinator dedicated to supporting grassroots innovators.

He works with institutions like TSIC, ni-msme, and NIRDPR to provide guidance, resources, and mentorship.

His mission is to reach innovators directly and help them turn ideas into real solutions.

Our Core Values

Our Values

At Cornerstone, we value innovation, accessibility, and practical learning. We focus on reaching grassroots innovators and enabling them to turn ideas into real-world solutions through hands-on support. By fostering collaboration and staying impact-driven, we aim to create meaningful change in communities.

Mission

To identify, support, and nurture innovators by providing practical guidance, technical skills, and real-world exposure. We aim to bridge the gap between ideas and execution through hands-on learning, mentorship, and innovation-driven initiatives.

Vision

To build a strong innovation ecosystem where every aspiring creator, especially from grassroots and rural backgrounds, has the opportunity, support, and resources to transform ideas into impactful solutions.
